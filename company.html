HISTORICAL HIGHLIGHTS
I
The company started from a humble beginning with one tipper truck which was hired to mining service contractors. Hiring service was rendered to a small scale mining firm, E K Agyemang Mining Company Limited being the first Mining Firm the company worked for. We have developed to a level where we now own Twelve Tipper Trucks ,Gold Washing Plant ,One Dozer , One Grader and six excavators which are hired to companies working in the mining industry. We are currently involved in small scale mining activities at Amansie Central District in Ashanti Region.

Objectives

Road and Building Construction ,Civil Engineering Works ,Mining Construction,Trading in General Goods, Transport Service, Import and Export of General Goods.
